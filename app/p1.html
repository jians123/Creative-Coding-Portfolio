Project #1 Interactive Typography

"Happy," "New," and "Year" are the terms I've picked. Because I am now celebrating the Chinese Lunar New Year, which is a highly important celebration in China, I picked these phrases. As a result, when I was given the task, I decided to use these lines to express my hope for the new year, which is to come across things that will make me happy this year and to start fresh.

Link to Code Sketch

Joy is a great sensation that comes to mind when you hear the word "Happy." People are glad when they come across items that might make them joyful, like as winning a game or being admitted to their preferred institution. When you click the mouse, the enormous and little letters of "Happy" falls heavily to the ground in my design. It implies that the things that satisfy you might appear in unexpected ways, such as "Happy" and suprise unexpectedly coming to you.

Link to Code Sketch

The term "New" refers to anything that has never existed before. People are frequently surprised and perplexed by new things. The phrases in red or white in my design emerge from the depths and move towards the foreground against a black background.(The color scheme is inspired by The New School logo) It provides the spectator an impression of being in a contemporary, crowded environment. This is extremely similar to how individuals felt when they first encountered something new. They will be dazzled since they are unsure how to deal with the new situations. At the same time, kids experience a certain type of beauty and excitement that comes with "new" things.

Link to Code Sketch

The term "Year" is used to indicate the passage of time. A year is a fixed span of time. A year will be followed by another, in a sort of cyclical pattern. This attitude is reflected in my design. The large white "Year" in the first and third lines, as well as in "earyea," indicates that a year follows a year. Then there are little letters arranged in a spiral pattern. The spiral rotates, much like time does, yet it is always a spiral, never ending and repeated. This might demonstrate the philosophy of infinite time. All of the elements in this image are dynamic, implying that time is continually moving by and that one year will be rapidly replaced by another.

Reflection

I've gained a better grasp of typography as a result of this project. We now employ technology to create new words. It's a contemporary approach, yet ideas are always at the heart of design. I have to tie my design to the meaning of the phrase while designing typography, which needs ingenuity. I've also witnessed the wonders of technology. The design process may be made more productive and engaging by using the modular and parameterized paradigm.
